Welcome to Hot Moths

Researchers at the UK Centre for Ecology & Hydrology have developed a sophisitcated AI algorithm for translating moth communications into human readable text.

On first contact, the moths expressed their deep concern about their poor reputation as 'carpet eaters' and 'hole makers'.

We have worked with the moth community develop a sophisitcated platform to help humans match with moth counterparts.

We hope this will usher in a new era of moth-human relations.
